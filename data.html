data

The data layer manages the storage and retrieval of data within a system, acting as a bridge between the application's business logic and the underlying data storage mechanisms like databases or file systems.

It includes components like database management systems, data access objects or repositories, ORM frameworks, caching mechanisms, and data validation logic.

Its primary role is to ensure efficient and reliable data management while abstracting the complexities of data storage from the rest of the application.
